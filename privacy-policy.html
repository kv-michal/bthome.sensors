Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BTHome Sensors is handled by its developer, Michal Koval, and explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights under applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR.

1. About the App

BTHome Sensors uses Bluetooth to scan nearby devices that implement the BTHome protocol. Sensor data is displayed in graphs and stored locally on your device for a user-defined period. The app may optionally receive data in the background when enabled by the user.

2. Data Storage and Processing

All sensor data and history are stored only on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to the developer’s servers.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time using the in-app controls.

3. Data Collected Automatically

The app itself does not collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or user-generated content.

Limited technical data may be processed by third-party services used to improve app stability:

  • Device identifiers (such as an anonymous device ID) are used by analytics and crash reporting tools.
  • Crash diagnostics may be collected when the app crashes to help identify and fix bugs.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Expo – application framework and runtime
  • PostHog – anonymous usage analytics
  • Sentry – crash reporting and diagnostics

These services may process limited technical information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Permissions

BTHome Sensors may request the following permissions:

  • Bluetooth – to scan and communicate with BTHome devices
  • Location – required by iOS for Bluetooth scanning
  • Notifications – to optionally notify you about sensor events

Permissions are used only for their intended functionality and can be revoked at any time in iOS system settings.

6. Advertising and Tracking

The app does not display advertisements and does not use tracking for advertising purposes.

7. Children’s Privacy

BTHome Sensors is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Rights (EU / GDPR)

As an EU user, you have the right to access, delete, or restrict the use of your data. Since all user data is stored locally on your device, these rights can be exercised directly within the app.
